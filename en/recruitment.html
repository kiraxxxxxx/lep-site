LEP is an Osaka University startup dedicated to the research, development, and social implementation of self-luminous plants. Our technology holds the potential to create sustainable lighting, spatial experiences, and entirely new ways of living in cities.

What We Value

What We Value

Curiosity

The curiosity to venture into uncharted territory. The ability to enjoy unprecedented challenges at the intersection of life sciences and technology.

Collaboration

Researchers, engineers, designers, business—new value is born precisely because our team brings together diverse expertise.

Sustainability

Creating a culture of light that does not rely on electricity. That commitment extends to our daily research—sustainable making that considers both environment and society.
