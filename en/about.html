About LEP

Purpose

Healing the Earth
with Nature's Wisdom.

LEP (Light Emitting Plants) unlocks the innate power of bioluminescence through science, delivering a new culture of light that does not depend on electricity. Toward a future where light in harmony with nature gently accompanies both cities and everyday life.

Vision

A society in harmony with nature,
illuminated by living plants.

Bringing a new light in harmony with the environment that requires no electricity.
Transforming the concept of illumination in urban and living spaces.

Market Expansion

Three Business Domains

LEP's self-luminous technology is advancing social implementation across three domains: spaces, lifestyle, and urban infrastructure.

01 — Space Design

Space Design

Overwhelming impact and emotion through total immersion.

Large-scale installations for hotels, commercial facilities, and weddings. Creative expression freed from power and wiring constraints delivers both "buzz-worthy attraction" and "environmental contribution."

02 — Houseplant, Flower Gift

Lifestyle

A new emotional experience for everyday life.

Glowing houseplants and luminous flowers—roses, carnations, orchids. Through "life's light," delivering moments of tranquility and wonder to your daily life.

03 — Lighting Infrastructure

Lighting Infrastructure

Toward an environmentally harmonious future city.

Implementing "zero electricity, CO₂-negative" lighting using self-luminous trees, dramatically reducing energy costs and environmental impact for next-generation infrastructure.
