Light Emitting Plants

Healing the Earth
with Nature's Wisdom.

LEP develops proprietary self-luminous and high-brightness technologies to bring electricity-free, low-impact "self-glowing plants" to society.

Vision

A society in harmony with nature,
illuminated by living plants.

Bringing a new light in harmony with the environment that requires no electricity. Transforming the concept of illumination in urban and living spaces.

Core Technology

Proprietary Luminescent
Protein Technology

Autonomous Luminescence

Self-Luminescence

No substrate addition required

Redesigning bioluminescent mushroom systems within plants. Building autonomous circuits that glow continuously without external substrate addition.

High Brightness

High Luminosity

Outstanding brightness enhancement

Our proprietary "Nano-lantern" technology fuses luminescent enzymes with fluorescent proteins, optimizing energy transfer to achieve 20x brightness over conventional methods.

Multi-Color

Multi-Color

Cutting-edge color diversification

By precisely controlling light wavelengths, we have achieved approximately 20 color variations in luminescent proteins, opening infinite possibilities for future illumination and landscape design.

Market Expansion

From extraordinary immersion
to everyday infrastructure.

01 — Space Design

Space Design Business

Overwhelming impact and emotion through total immersion.

We provide large-scale installations using luminous plants for hotel lounges, commercial illumination, weddings, and concerts. By liberating creative expression from the constraints of power sources and wiring, we simultaneously deliver "buzz-worthy customer attraction" and "meaningful environmental contribution."

02 — Houseplant, Flower Gift

Lifestyle Business

A new emotional experience that brings healing and color to everyday life.

We offer glowing houseplants for relaxation and luminous flowers—roses, carnations, orchids—for gift occasions. Through "life's light," we deliver moments of tranquility and wonder to your daily life.

03 — Lighting Infrastructure

Lighting Infrastructure Business

Toward an environmentally harmonious future city without electricity.

Long-term, we aim to socially implement "zero electricity, CO₂-negative" environmentally harmonious lighting using self-luminous trees as alternatives to current lighting fixtures, dramatically reducing energy costs and environmental impact for next-generation urban development.
